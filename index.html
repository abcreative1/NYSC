About Us

The National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) is a program established to foster unity, skill development, and national integration among Nigerian youth, Since its inception, NYSC has played a pivotal role in shaping young graduates into future leaders through service, cultural exchange, and community impact.

view more

How it works

Registration

Start your NYSC journey by completing the online registration and receiving your call-up latter. This is your first step toward national service

Orientation Camp

Experience three weeks of intensive training drills, leadership sessions, and cultural exchange. Meet fellow corps members, develop new skills, and prepare for your service year.

Primary Assignment

Deploy to your Place of Primary Assignment (PPA), where you'll contribute to community development while gaining real-world experience. This is your chance to make an impact!

Passing Out (POP)

After successfully completing your service year, celebrate your achievements at the Passing Out Parade and receive your NYSC discharge certificate - your badge of honor!
